winforms-ify (v0.4.0)

    - Make tabs more compact
    - Change some text rendering methods
    - Change tab length and height
    - Fix 'goofy' text rendering for the most part. Try to match GDI font styling as closely as possible
    - Re-arrange demos in general tab
    - Adjust window height/width defaults and minimums

- Stop auto-update notif from breaking the ui and requiring a force quit
